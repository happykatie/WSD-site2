Meet Your Local Dairy Farmers

As a multigenerational Florida dairy farming family, our mission is to produce superior quality, wholesome, farm-fresh dairy products from healthy cows. We are committed to using environmentally sustainable practices and state-of-the- art methods. We are also committed to promoting the education of the public about dairy farming and the nutritional benefits of "natures perfect food" - MILK!

Visit Our Farm!

Our family has been milking cows in Highlands County, Florida since 1973. Butler Oaks Farm is amongst the fewer than 150 dairies remaining in Florida. We are committed to educating the public about dairy farming and the nutritional benefits of milk. Our Farm Tours offer a unique experience and create memories that will last a lifetime.

On Our Tour You Will:

  1. Learn"Where Milk Comes From"
  2. Learn the benefits of a FRESH GRASS diet
  3. Learn how we practice sustainable farming
  4. "Taste the Difference" of truly fresh milk
  5. Take a hayride and conclude with bottle feeding a calf
